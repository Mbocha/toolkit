Managing journeys of your fleet vehicles can improve driver behavior and scheduling of vehicle use. It can also identify opportunities to downsize either the number of vehicles or the types of vehicles retained.

                        

Monitoring and On Board Diagnostics (OBD)

If driver behavior is monitored by an on-board computer, and if fuel-saving eco-driving behavior is rewarded, the long-term effects from a training will be greater. An OBD system checks vital engine functions, such as oil filters and ignition systems, and emission levels of the engine. The most common feature a driver sees is the “Check engine" lamp. With some extra equipment, an OBD system enables you to also monitor fuel economy and driving patterns with an on-board computer.

Trip sharing

Is every trip necessary? Can a trip be combined with another? Can better trip planning reduce ‘empty’ trips? Monitoring the objectives and schedules of planned trips can result in combining or, in some cases, avoiding trips.

For example, UNICEF found that around 60% of the trips to one destination happened within half an hour. In other words, they had multiple vehicles, each carrying only one passenger, driving back and forth to the same place at approximately the same time. With proper scheduling and management, most of these trip could be avoided.

A management system can also identify opportunities to reduce the size of the fleet. Before replacing a vehicle, ask:

  1. Given current and planned operations, is this vehicle still needed?
  2. If it is, then can it be replaced by a smaller, cheaper, more efficient vehicle?

One novel option is to combine fleets with other organizations. Using a shared fleet and installing a robust management system can provide significant cost and emission benefits through improved fuel consumption and smaller fleets, as described in the Case Study: UNICEF & Fleet Sharing.

UNHCR’s Global Fleet Management also shows the cost benefits of good fleet management; fleet size and costs decreased despite an increase in program activities.
